# Cafe / Mood Shot Focus

## Image Pattern

- coffee, dessert, table detail, or countertop composition
- strong light, texture, steam, or color contrast
- atmosphere matters as much as the object itself

## What attention should find

- the detail that gives the scene emotional texture
- the question a viewer starts asking after stopping on that detail
- a draft that writes from mood plus object, not object names alone

## Example Direction

Possible hook:
- the way light, texture, or one plated detail makes the scene feel more expensive or memorable

Likely viewer question:
- what exactly in this corner or dish makes the place feel worth visiting?

Draft direction:
- lead with the sensory detail
- connect it to mood
- end with a line that feels personal instead of review-like

## Why this matters

Mood-driven cafe posts usually do better when the caption chooses one image-led sensation to unpack instead of listing every item on the table.
