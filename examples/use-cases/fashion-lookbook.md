# Fashion / Outfit Focus

## Image Pattern

- mirror selfie or outfit photo
- one standout styling move
- background is useful, but not the main reason people stop

## What attention should find

- the one styling decision that makes the photo feel intentional
- the question viewers are most likely to ask about that decision
- a caption draft that starts from the hook instead of summarizing the whole outfit

## Example Direction

Possible hook:
- the contrast between a quiet base outfit and one high-attention accent

Likely viewer question:
- what makes this outfit feel sharp without looking overdone?

Draft direction:
- start from the accent piece or silhouette choice
- explain why it changes the whole look
- keep the tone specific and easy to edit into a final post

## Why this matters

Fashion photos often fail when the caption repeats everything visible in the image. `attention` should isolate the one decision worth writing into.
