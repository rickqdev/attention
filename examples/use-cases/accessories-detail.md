# Accessories / Detail Focus

## Image Pattern

- close-up of nails, jewelry, charms, or bag details
- small object with strong contrast or unusual texture
- supporting colors or styling that reinforce the focal detail

## What attention should find

- the detail that pulls the eye before the rest of the frame
- the exact curiosity it creates
- a draft that uses that curiosity as the opening move

## Example Direction

Possible hook:
- an unusual nail charm or jewelry shape that steals focus from the full look

Likely viewer question:
- how was that detail made, found, or styled?

Draft direction:
- name the detail early
- explain why it grabs attention
- let the rest of the outfit or mood act as support, not the headline
