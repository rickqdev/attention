# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want `attention` to feel useful, direct, and respectful for creators and developers from different backgrounds.

Expected behavior:
- be constructive and specific
- assume good intent
- critique ideas and implementations, not people
- keep examples safe to share in public

Unacceptable behavior:
- harassment or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private assets without permission
- spam, abuse, or intentionally misleading claims about the product

## Scope

This applies in issues, discussions, pull requests, releases, and linked community spaces for the project.

## Reporting

If something needs moderation, open a private report through the repository owner contact channel or flag it in GitHub so it can be reviewed.
